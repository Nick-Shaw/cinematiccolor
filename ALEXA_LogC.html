ALEXA LogC

LogC is the encoding used in the ARRI ALEXA series of cameras, including the AMIRA. It is used together with the ALEXA Wide Gamut primaries and a white point of D65. The exact encoding curve varies slightly with the Exposure Index (EI) set on the camera, keeping 18 if L > cut: V = c × log10(a × L + b) + d else: V = e × L + f

At EI800 the values of the constants are:

cut = 0.010591 a = 5.555556 b = 0.052272 c = 0.247190 d = 0.385537 e = 5.367655 ï¿¼ f = 0.092809

The decoding function is:

if V > e × cut + f: L = (10(V − d) / c − b) / a else: (V − f) / e
