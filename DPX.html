DPX

DPX is a SMPTE standardized image format that is commonly used in the motion-picture industry. While the DPX format supports a variety of pixel types including float32, it is most commonly associated with uncompressed 10 bit and 16 bit unsigned integer RGB imagery. Unlike EXR - which is synonymous with scene-referred linear colorimetry, DPX does not have a canonical color space. It is common for DPX to store any number of integer camera log encodings, in addition to broadcast-ready Rec. 709.
